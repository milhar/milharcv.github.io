Welcome to S & A CONSTRUCTION (PVT) LTD

Specialized in Pre-fabricated Steel Buildings, Structures, Civil Engineering and All Kinds of Commercial Buildings, Domestic Buildings, Architectural Design.

S & A Construction commenced functioning in 2006 and registered as a limited liability company. S & A has over ten years of experience in the construction industry and has wide experience in the field of construction of fabricated steel Buildings. We have been providing our local clientele with high quality technologically advanced Building solutions. We consider our clientele as our partners and our skilled workforce which unite to provide the strength for the rapid growth.

We have now added the steel Fabrication workshop manufacturing all necessary components for buildings and gates, grill and other accessories. In addition we stock building materials and quality cement blocks to cater to our customer needs.

With the organizational capability and skills developed in the construction industry, we are now capable to undertake the construction of RCC Buildings, Infra-structure Development works such as pipe laying, construction of retaining walls, road works etc.
